I started my tech career in Customer Support answering phones and emails while paying my way through college. During my time at Zynga, I've collected multiple studio awards and a department award in the Customer Support.

From there, I moved into Community Management and collected a few more studio awards. I've also dipped my hand into game design by making several successful and highly profitable in-game features. It was an awesome experience to see positive feedback from the players. I then discovered that I wanted to make epic things that people are going to enjoy.

I worked closely with Marketing to incorporate new features and content to the site keep it fresh and engaging. Then I moved onto our E-commerce platform team where I create new and complelling features. I played an important part in the creation and launch of the US-Chinese site on LeEco.com.
