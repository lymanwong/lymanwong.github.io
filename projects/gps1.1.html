What is pair programming?

It's a technique of team prgramming which 2 programmers work togther at one workstasion. One programmer would be designated as a driver and another programmer would be a navigator.

2 programmers switch thier roles frequently.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

Based on Takkaki's experience, collaborating with another programmer builds a relationship and it is more efficient than working alone. It also keeps the team focused on the project at hand.

Compare Driver and Navigator

  • Driver
    1. The person who writes the code
    2. Focusus how to execute the task
  • Navigator
    1. The person who reviews the driver's code
    2. Makes broad stragetic decision
    3. Makes the driver concentrate on the task
