How can I use my thinking and learning style to my advantage at DevBootcamp?
I feel that the Concreate Random "likes" is spot on for me. During my homework, I go with my instincts and use the trial and error method to figure things out. Although I do not like to compete with others, I'd rather be the one working on things on my own with little guidance. I need to keep in mind that there are others that I can leverage experiences from and keep in mind that they're there to help. I also have problems with quality control because when I complete something, I do not go over them; which I should. I have to keep the "measure twice and cut once" method in mind if I'm going to bue successful.
