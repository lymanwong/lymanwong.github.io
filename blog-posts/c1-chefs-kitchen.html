My response to Shereef's Fireside Chat - 8.11.2014

Based on Shereef's Fireside Chat, my impression of the DBC's experience is that the experience is a sprint. The good thing is that we will nto be going on this journey alone. I really like that Shereef discussed how we are all starting at different experience levels. I do not have a CS degree or even a CS background. I have fear that I will not be able to positively contribute to my group. Well, shit. I have to work through that somehow and know that everyone is here to help. For me, I go to the gym to work that business out in my head.

As Sherref's conversation continued, I felt a bit more at ease about how I could let my team down. I also felt more optimistic that I can kill it with a lot of hard work. I look forward to pair with other students and teachers as I know I have a lot to learn. Above all, I feel a bit nervous/scared and excited about it. I'm boldly going where I have never gone before!

I do have a question: If some students have been accepted multiple months ahead, why can't we start before? I was really excited and I wanted to start right away.

Overall, I really enjoyed the video and I really benefited from it.
